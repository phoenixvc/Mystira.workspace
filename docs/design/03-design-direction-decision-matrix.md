# Mystira Design Direction Decision Matrix

## Purpose

This matrix provides a structured framework for evaluating UI design directions against Mystira's core product goals:

- Narrative storytelling
- Magical atmosphere
- Child-friendly clarity
- Long-term design system scalability
- Product usability

---

## Evaluation Criteria

| Dimension               | Weight | Description                          |
|-------------------------|--------|--------------------------------------|
| Narrative immersion     | 0.20   | Feels like a story world             |
| Brand uniqueness        | 0.15   | Distinct from SaaS / crypto UI       |
| Child readability       | 0.15   | Clear for younger audiences          |
| Product usability       | 0.15   | Navigation clarity                   |
| System scalability      | 0.10   | Token + component system             |
| Visual hierarchy        | 0.10   | Scanability                          |
| Emotional resonance     | 0.10   | Delight / magic                      |
| Engineering simplicity  | 0.05   | Ease of implementation               |

---

## Score Matrix (1–5 scale)

| Design Direction        | Narrative | Brand | Child | Usability | System | Hierarchy | Emotion | Eng | **Weighted** |
|-------------------------|-----------|-------|-------|-----------|--------|-----------|---------|-----|-------------|
| SaaS Purple (A)         | 2         | 2     | 3     | 5         | 5      | 4         | 2       | 5   | **3.35**    |
| Cosmic Vault (B)        | 4         | 4     | 2     | 3         | 3      | 4         | 4       | 3   | **3.55**    |
| Mystira Archive (C)     | 5         | 5     | 4     | 4         | 4      | 5         | 5       | 4   | **4.55**    |

---

## Decision

**Mystira Archive direction wins decisively.**

### Role Assignment

| Design Direction   | Recommended Role                                    |
|--------------------|-----------------------------------------------------|
| Concept A (SaaS)   | Internal admin, creator tools, analytics dashboards |
| Concept B (Vault)  | Premium artifact screens, achievements, collectibles |
| Concept C (Mystira) | Primary product identity, story exploration, lore   |

---

## Decision Rules

1. **Player-facing screens** must use Concept C as the foundation
2. **Admin and creator tools** may use Concept A patterns for clarity
3. **Rare artifact / achievement moments** may borrow Concept B accents (gold glow, dark cosmic surfaces)
4. **All directions** must share the same semantic token system
5. **No raw hex values** in any component — tokens only
