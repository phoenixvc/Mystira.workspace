# Development Principles (for judges and refiners)

- Emotional safety: conflict is mild and resolvable.
- Agency: choices feel meaningful (not cosmetic).
- Clarity: descriptions and stakes are easy to understand.
- Growth: characters learn and adapt in age-appropriate ways.
- Kindness and repair: when mistakes happen, show repair and responsibility.
- Curiosity and wonder: keep the world engaging without fear-based hooks.
